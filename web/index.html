02

competitive intelligence

01

Auto-Discover Rivals

Tell GhostStream a region and it finds the competitors who actually threaten you — each analyzed for their strength, the products of yours they threaten, and a clear threat ranking. Add the relevant ones in a click.

02

Their Real Product Lineup

Paste a competitor's website and GhostStream pulls their homepage as intel, then maps their actual product line — grounded on their own pages, never a rival's — against yours.

03

Battlecards & Matchups

Generate company-wide and product-by-product battlecards from your intel and theirs — where they win, where you win, and the exact line to use. Always current, always on hand.
