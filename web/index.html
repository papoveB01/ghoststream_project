how it works

No imports to babysit, no playbooks to write. Sign up, point DealScope at your business, and the intelligence starts compounding the same day.

  1. teach

    It learns your business

    Connect your CRM and calendar, or just your website. DealScope builds your foundation — products, positioning, buyer personas, ideal customer profile — in about a minute.

questions

Good questions, straight answers.

Does DealScope connect to my CRM?

Yes — connect HubSpot in one paste to bring your companies and contacts in, no CSV exports. Salesforce, Zoho and more are on the way.

Does the AI join my meetings?

Yes. It joins your Zoom, Microsoft Teams or Google Meet calls and captures everything so your reps stay present — with the follow-up ready about a minute after the call ends.

Is my data private?

Strict tenant isolation, encryption in transit and at rest, email-verified onboarding and per-tenant recording controls. Your data never trains anyone else's model.

Do I need a credit card to start?

No. The Free plan needs no card and lets you try the core platform. Upgrade or add credits whenever you're ready.

What happens when I hit a monthly limit?

You're never silently cut off. Buy a one-time credit pack on any plan, or on Pro simply keep going at $2.50 per extra call. Limits reset on the 1st of each month.

How do seats work?

Plans include seats (Starter 1, Pro 2). Each extra seat is a flat monthly price and grows your research and call allowances — so a bigger team gets a bigger plan automatically.
